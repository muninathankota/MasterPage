About the College

Established in 1994, St. Joseph’s College of Engineering has evolved into one of Tamil Nadu’s leading institutions, offering 11 UG and 3 PG programs with a strong commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and research.

The institution holds Autonomous Status (UGC), is affiliated to Anna University, and is accredited with NAAC A+ Grade and NBA accreditation for multiple programs.
